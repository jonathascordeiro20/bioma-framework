B.I.O.M.A.: Biologically-Inspired Orchestration of Mutating Agents

A framework for dynamic neural-agent orchestration and a discipline for epistemic honesty in bio-inspired AI

Jonathas Cordeiro

Independent Researcher · jonathas.cordeiro2023@gmail.com  ·  author & affiliation are placeholders to finalize before submission

No empirical results by design Reference target: Windows 11 · Python 3.12 · PyTorch (CPU-only) Manuscript v1.0
Integrity note. This is a Stage-1 registered report. It specifies architecture, methodology, and a pre-registered evaluation protocol with falsifiable hypotheses. It reports no measured performance, survival rates, or convergence curves, because none have been run. Abstract

Contemporary “autonomous agent” frameworks are predominantly static software wrappers around a frozen model, and the biological vocabulary attached to them (“organisms,” “evolution,” “self-healing”) is rarely grounded in a concrete mechanism, inviting overclaiming. We present B.I.O.M.A. (Biologically-Inspired Orchestration of Mutating Agents), a PyTorch framework in which agents are lightweight neural modules organized in a runtime-mutable directed acyclic graph (DAG) that expands and contracts during inference. B.I.O.M.A. realizes four capabilities under an explicit biological-to-engineering mapping: (i) mitosis, runtime growth by embedding-space clustering plus state-dictionary duplication, in the Mixture-of-Experts / dynamic-network lineage; (ii) apoptosis, structural pruning with knowledge consolidation and deterministic memory reclamation; (iii) a hormonal bus, an in-process shared-latent blackboard read by cosine attention with eventual-consistency semantics; and (iv) an energy homeostasis model, a non-conservative compute budget with uncertainty regularization. Our second, equally central contribution is methodological: a three-level metaphor→mechanism honesty taxonomy (N1 concrete / N2 analogy / N3 narrative) that disciplines every claim and targets the field's chronic overclaiming problem. We formalize falsifiable, operational metrics—most notably a statistically defined Computational Survival Rate (CSR) with Wilson lower bounds—and pre-register a 2×2 factorial ablation on a synthetic benchmark with analytic ground truth, plus an identifiability analysis tying expected benefit to a controllable coupling parameter. We conclude with an optional evolutionary auto-refactoring extension whose security and reproducibility boundaries we make explicit.

1Introduction

The dominant pattern for “agentic” AI couples a fixed neural network to an orchestration layer that routes text through tools and other model instances. The topology of computation is static: the same parameters and the same graph serve a trivial prompt and a multi-domain crisis alike. In parallel, a large body of work has shown that the graph itself can be a first-class, adaptive object—networks can grow while preserving function4,5,6, route computation conditionally through specialized experts2,3, be searched or evolved as architectures7,8,16,17, and coordinate through shared external memory rather than message passing11,13. B.I.O.M.A. asks: what does an agent framework look like if the population of agents and the communication substrate are themselves dynamical objects, created and destroyed in response to the cognitive load of the input?

Bio-inspiration is a double-edged sword. It is a rich source of mechanisms rooted in the artificial-life tradition26—morphogenesis10, stigmergy12, programmed cell death, neuroevolution18—but it is also a well-documented vector for scholarship that conflates a suggestive name with a demonstrated property22. A system that “undergoes mitosis” and “apoptosis” is easy to narrate and hard to falsify unless every term is pinned to a measurable operation. We therefore treat epistemic honesty not as a disclaimer but as a design constraint with its own artifacts and acceptance tests.

Contributions

  1. A framework (§3) for dynamic neural-agent orchestration: runtime DAG mitosis, apoptosis with deterministic memory reclamation, an in-process hormonal bus, and a non-conservative energy homeostasis model—each with contracts and gradient-safety invariants.
  2. A methodological discipline (§4): a three-level metaphor→mechanism taxonomy applied uniformly and itself subject to acceptance criteria (e.g., zero unqualified claims of “life”).
  3. A pre-registered protocol (§5): operational, falsifiable metrics; a 2×2 factorial ablation on a synthetic benchmark with analytic ground truth; identifiability analysis; a statistical plan with power, corrections, and stopping rules.
  4. An optional evolutionary extension (§6) for automated code refactoring, with explicit security (subprocess isolation, never exec) and reproducibility boundaries.

Scope. We deliberately do not report performance numbers because none have been measured; the reference implementation, built on PyTorch1, targets a CPU-only commodity host and the full protocol has not been executed. What we commit to here—architecture, honesty discipline, metrics, and analysis plan—is fixed in advance so a Stage-2 report can be judged against predictions rather than post-hoc rationalizations23,24.

2Related Work

Dynamic and growing networks. Net2Net4 introduced function-preserving transforms that widen or deepen a network while exactly preserving its function at expansion; progressive networks5 and dynamically expandable networks6 grow capacity for new tasks. HyperNetworks9 take a complementary route, generating a network's weights from a smaller meta-network. B.I.O.M.A. inherits the idea of runtime structural change but—crucially—its duplication is not a Net2Net identity guarantee; it is a state-dictionary deep-copy whose post-split behavioral equality is a copy sanity check, not an algebraic width-expansion identity.

Conditional computation and Mixture-of-Experts. Sparsely-gated MoE2 and Switch Transformers3 activate input-dependent sub-networks through a learned router with load-balancing. B.I.O.M.A.'s “stem cell” is a router that decides whether and how much to expand; its mitosis is MoE-style specialization by clustering, and we credit that lineage rather than claiming a novel growth primitive.

Architecture search and neuroevolution. NAS7,8 frames topology as a search problem; regularized evolution17 and NEAT16,18 evolve architectures and weights. Our optional extension (§6) sits in the genetic-improvement tradition19,20,21 applied to source code, not to network weights.

Morphogenesis versus routed growth. Growing Neural Cellular Automata10 are the most honest artificial-life reference for growth, but they are a distinct paradigm: emergent self-organization from a homogeneous local rule. B.I.O.M.A. grows by central router decision, not emergence; we cite NCA as a contrast and reserve “morphogenesis” and “emergence” for explicitly speculative future work.

Communication substrates. Rather than text-API message passing, B.I.O.M.A. uses a shared latent field read by attention—an in-process descendant of blackboard architectures11, stigmergy12, and differentiable external memory13,14. We do not claim “real-time, overhead-free” communication; we claim the elimination of serialization cost under eventual-consistency semantics.

Pruning, distillation, adaptive compute. Apoptosis draws on classical pruning31,32 and knowledge distillation33; the energy budget is in the spirit of adaptive computation time15. Our contribution is their orchestrated integration under one honest lifecycle.

Scholarship norms. We adopt community reproducibility guidance24,25, the registered-report format23, and take seriously the critique of overclaiming22. The CPU-only target is a deliberate nod to the hardware-lottery argument27.

3The B.I.O.M.A. Framework

B.I.O.M.A. is organized around five modules: organism_core (the passive neural unit), mitosis_engine (the fission controller), hormonal_bus (the shared latent substrate), simulation_harness (the benchmark and judge), and an optional evolutionary_coder (§6).

3.1  Design principles

  1. Dual naming. Every entity carries a biological name and an engineering name; the engineering name is authoritative for code, logs, and metrics.
  2. Gradient safety by isolation. Structural mutation occurs only between backward passes; children are born detached, with disjoint autograd graphs.
  3. Determinism where it counts. Validation runs execute serially for logical reproducibility; bit-exact numerics are best-effort only.
  4. Device honesty. The reference target is CPU-only; empty_cache is guarded by cuda.is_available(); no result depends on a specific accelerator.

3.2  Organism core: genome, mutation, energy

An organism is an nn.Module augmented with a genome: id, generation, parent id, specialization label, mutation seed, birth step, device, and an architecture signature (a canonical hash of topology, shapes, dtypes). Replication is a deep-copy of the state dictionary; independence is asserted by distinct storage pointers. Mutation perturbs each layer with layer-relative Gaussian noise,

θ′ℓ = θℓ + εℓ, εℓ ∼ N(0, (m·(std(θℓ)+ε))2)
(1)

drawn from a dedicated torch.Generator for reproducibility, with a mutability mask freezing normalization parameters and a finiteness-rejection step. Energy accounting (§3.5) is attached as a forward hook under no_grad, so it never perturbs gradients.

3.3  Mitosis: semantic divergence and clustered fission

Given a scenario embedded by a shared encoder Φ into points in ℝd, the stem-cell router computes a scalar semantic divergence

D = Σk=14 wk sk, Σk wk = 1, sk ∈ [0,1]
(2)

a convex combination of four normalized signals: cluster-mass entropy, a silhouette score30 computed on the candidate partition (not before it, to avoid circularity), an inter/intra variance ratio, and mean pairwise distance. Fission fires under hysteresis: the population expands when D exceeds an upper threshold τ↑ (with an EMA and a cooldown), and contraction uses a lower threshold τ↓ < τ↑. The number of children is chosen by scanning candidate k and maximizing Quality(k) − λk; if no k > 1 beats k = 1, no mitosis occurs. Children inherit the parent's weights plus a specialization bias toward their cluster centroid, injected through a conditioning context rather than by overwriting weights.

Honesty note

The post-split equality ‖fafter − fbefore‖ < 10−5 (before adaptation) is a deep-copy sanity check, not the Net2Net function-preservation identity. We additionally require an effective differentiation test after adaptation, so mitosis is shown to do work rather than to be expensive theater.

3.4  Hormonal bus: shared latent field with attention reads

The bus is a tensor M of S slots (one per live cell). A cell secretes by an EMA write with norm clamping and non-finite sanitization; it senses by true cosine attention over occupied, non-stale slots:

aj = softmaxj( (1/τ) · ⟨q, kj⟩ / (‖q‖ ‖kj‖) ), r = Σj aj vj
(3)

Temporal dissipation is applied at each tick to all occupied slots, so stale signals decay and attention can discard them. Concurrency uses versioned snapshot isolation (copy under lock, compute outside it) and an explicit differentiability policy (non-differentiable by default; stop-gradient on keys in the opt-in mode).

Honesty note

Communication is eventually consistent: a read observes the last consolidated tick, not the instant a peer wrote. The gain over text-API agents is the elimination of serialization, at the cost of lock synchronization and tick latency.

3.5  Energy homeostasis

Each cell carries a scalar state in a float64 buffer updated under no_grad:

Ei(t+1) = clamp( Ei(t) − λbasal Ei(t) − ΔEwork − κ Hnorm + η·max(0, Δutil), Emin, Emax )
(4)

where ΔEwork is an analytic FLOP/token proxy, Hnorm = H/log K is normalized Shannon uncertainty, and regeneration rewards progress (sub-domain loss reduction), never mere activity.

Honesty note

The sources, sinks, and clamp in Eq. (4) make the system non-conservative by design. The auditable property is accounting consistency (no double counting, no float drift, Ei ≥ 0), not physical conservation. “Entropy” here is Shannon uncertainty, a chosen regularizer, not thermodynamic entropy.

3.6  Apoptosis, lifecycle, concurrency

Termination is a state machine alive→dying→transferring→dead triggered by an OR of four conditions with hysteresis (except energy exhaustion, immediate): task solved, energy exhausted, low marginal contribution, senescence. Before release, the cell transfers its representation to the parent by utility-weighted interpolation or embedding distillation33, with a pre-merge snapshot for rollback. Cleanup follows a canonical order: drop references → detach → move to CPU → gc.collect() → empty_cache() (CUDA-guarded), verified by a leak auditor. A topological scheduler ensures a parent reduces only after all children apoptose. The concurrency model is cooperative single-thread: bus coordination on the event loop, with the only true parallelism being per-cell computation over disjoint tensors; we state plainly that under the GIL single-process parallelism is bounded by Amdahl's law.

4A Metaphor→Mechanism Discipline

Our methodological contribution is a uniform discipline for naming. Every term is assigned an engineering mechanism, a technical family, and a honesty level:

N1

Concrete. A verifiable mechanism with a measurable operation.

N2

Analogy. A defensible functional analogy, not a literal claim.

N3

Narrative. Creative license with no mechanical referent; permitted only when explicitly flagged.

Table 1 is the canonical dictionary and the single source of truth for terminology. It is not decoration: the protocol (§5) includes an acceptance test requiring zero unqualified claims of “life,” “consciousness,” “emergence,” or “thermodynamics.”

5Pre-Registered Evaluation Protocol

We fix, in advance, the benchmark, metrics, design, statistical plan, and hypotheses. No aspect may be altered post hoc without disclosure.

5.1  Synthetic benchmark with analytic ground truth

For K latent domains we draw orthonormal prototypes P via a QR factorization, generate point clouds, and define a coupling matrix C normalized so the spectral radius ρ(γC) < 1. The analytic global solution is

S★ = (I − γC)−1 P
(5)

held detached and never observed by any organism. The coupling parameter γ controls how much the domains must be solved jointly: at γ→0 a monolithic solver suffices; as γ grows, coordination becomes necessary.

5.2  Operational metrics

Computational Survival Rate (CSR). For a cell c,

survive(c) = healthy_fwd ∧ ¬nan_inf ∧ transfer_ok ∧ clean_apoptosis ∧ ¬leak ∧ ¬race; CSR = (1/N) Σc 1[survive(c)]
(6)

Because CSR is a proportion over a stochastic process, acceptance is statistical: the Wilson28 lower bound of CSR at confidence 1−α must exceed a pre-declared threshold, or CSR = 1 conditioned on a second, tolerance-free oracle (the gc-object cross-check) classifying every sub-unity termination as genuine necrosis. “Survival” is explicitly not the absence of apoptosis: apoptosis is healthy and expected.

Convergence, decision fidelity, identifiability. Convergence is cos(ŝ, S★). Mitosis decision fidelity is a confusion matrix: firing on multi-domain and suppressing on uni-domain scenarios, with precision/recall above a floor. For identifiability we report the trivial baseline cos(ŝ, p) alongside cos(ŝ, S★) and sweep γ; if both are high the scenario is trivial and reported as such.

Memory-leak criterion (CPU-aware). Rather than “return to baseline ±1%” (fragile under allocator noise), the criterion is the absence of an upward trend in a soak test (slope b ≤ bmax, p ≥ 0.05) plus a tolerance-free gc count of live organisms returning to zero.

5.3  Design & statistical plan

The core design is a 2×2 factorial: mitosis {on, off} × hormonal bus {on, off}, with R replicates over a seed grid. Controls include a monolithic solver, a communication-free swarm, and—critically—adversarial scenarios where splitting is wrong, so the benchmark cannot be won by indiscriminate fission. We pre-commit to Welch's t / Mann–Whitney per normality; effect sizes (Cohen's d / Cliff's δ) with 95% bootstrap intervals; Holm–Bonferroni29 family-wise correction; and an a-priori power analysis sizing R for power ≥ 0.8. Validation runs use max_workers=1 (serial, logically deterministic); tolerances are empirical (10−4–10−3, not 10−5).

5.4  Hypotheses

H1 Mitosis utility

On multi-domain scenarios, enabling mitosis increases cos(ŝ, S★) over the monolithic baseline, and the margin increases with coupling γ.

H2 Bus utility

Enabling the hormonal bus increases convergence over a communication-free swarm, with the largest effect at high γ.

H3 Decision fidelity

The mitosis router fires on multi-domain and suppresses on uni-domain scenarios with precision and recall above the declared floor.

H4 Survival

The Wilson lower bound of CSR meets the declared threshold with no unrecovered anomaly and no memory-leak trend.

H5 Effective differentiation

After adaptation, sibling cells diverge and specialize (inter-sibling distance and per-domain loss reduction exceed a floor), i.e., mitosis performs work.

A null result on H1 or H2—mitosis or communication failing to help—is a valid, publishable outcome and would itself constrain the design space.

6Optional Extension: Evolutionary Auto-Refactoring

As a capability extension atop the frozen framework, the evolutionary_coder applies genetic-improvement search19,20,21 to refactor a target Python function, reusing the orchestration, lifecycle, and telemetry machinery but operating on programs, not tensors. Four honesty corrections are mandatory and are themselves acceptance criteria:

  1. No gradients. Refactoring is discrete, non-differentiable search; “dropped gradients during dynamic execution” is a category error. Static audit forbids any autograd reference in this module.
  2. Evolution does not discover a complexity class. Turning an O(N2) sort into O(N log N) does not emerge from random mutation; the gain comes from an AST transform already in the catalog or from an LLM proposal (knowledge, not natural selection). Each variant is labeled by origin; success is measured as latency reduction vs. a baseline, never narrated as emergent discovery.
  3. exec does not isolate. Variants run only in a resource-limited subprocess (setrlimit / Windows Job Objects, hard timeout, no network); in-process exec/eval of untrusted code is forbidden.
  4. “Reverse distillation” is pattern harvesting. The winning transform is promoted to the deterministic catalog; no network “learns the concept of clean code.”

Fitness is lexicographic: correctness as a hard gate (any failing test ⇒ fitness 0), then median latency over R repetitions, then memory delta. Two separated paths preserve the framework's reproducibility decision: an AST-deterministic path (in the validated set) and a quarantined, explicitly non-reproducible LLM path (excluded from statistical validation).

7Threats to Validity

Construct. “Energy” is a compute proxy, not a physical quantity; biological terms are scaffolds (Table 1). Semantic divergence depends on the encoder Φ; a poor Φ can mask or fabricate multi-domain structure. We freeze Φ's contract in advance and report sensitivity.

Internal. Asynchronous execution is a source of nondeterminism; we neutralize it for validation by serial execution and a canonical reduction order, and audit for read-after-write races directly.

External. The benchmark is synthetic, models are small, and the host is CPU-only. Results—if obtained—may not transfer to large-scale/GPU regimes or real workloads; we make no scaling claims beyond the tested regime27.

Statistical. Multiple comparisons are corrected29; replicate counts are sized by power analysis; tolerances are empirical, not aspirational.

8Ethical and Epistemic Considerations

The primary ethical risk of this line of work is not physical but rhetorical: presenting a Mixture-of-Experts router with pruning as an “autonomous digital life form” misleads readers and erodes trust in the field22. Our N1/N2/N3 discipline and its zero-unqualified-claim acceptance test are a direct, checkable countermeasure. The optional evolutionary extension executes generated code; we contain this strictly in an isolated subprocess with resource limits and no network, and treat all generated code as untrusted. We foresee no dual-use concern beyond standard code-execution hygiene.

9Conclusion and Future Work

B.I.O.M.A. reframes an agent framework as a dynamical system: a population of neural modules that grows and prunes itself in a runtime-mutable DAG, coordinated through a shared latent field and bounded by a compute budget. Its mechanisms are not new in isolation—they are borrowed, with credit, from MoE, dynamic networks, blackboard systems, pruning, and distillation—but their honest integration under a single lifecycle, and the discipline that keeps the biological narrative from outrunning the engineering, are the contribution. The pre-registered protocol commits us to be judged by predictions. The natural next step is the Stage-2 report: execute the 2×2 ablation and the soak test, and report—whatever the outcome—the CSR, the convergence-versus-γ curves, and the mitosis confusion matrix, including any refutation of H1–H5. Longer-term: a genuinely emergent (NCA-style) growth rule as an explicitly labeled branch, and a GPU study to test whether the orchestration survives contact with real parallelism.

Reproducibility statement

All hypotheses, metrics, and analyses are fixed in this Stage-1 document. Planned Stage-2 artifacts: seeded, versioned configuration; a declared births denominator N; serial validation for logical reproducibility; the synthetic-benchmark generator with its coupling sweep; and the leak-auditor and race-probe harnesses. Bit-exact numerical reproducibility is documented as best-effort under serial execution only. Code and logs will be released with the Stage-2 report.
